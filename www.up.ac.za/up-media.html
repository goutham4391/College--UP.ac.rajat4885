UP Media

Welcome to the University of Pretoria. We’re home to some of the world’s most notable experts in a variety of academic disciplines across all nine faculties and our business school, GIBS. We do research that’s focussed on changing lives for the better, and we’re on the cutting edge of scientific endeavour.

We’re a diverse and dynamic community focussed on providing the very best education and support structures for all our students—to help them make a success of their lives not only academically, but through work-readiness and entrepreneurship programmes which prepare them for the world beyond university.

We are one of the largest research universities in South Africa, and are committed to making a positive impact through our research, community engagement, student success, and economic impact.

We regularly host events, exhibitions, and conferences, and produce impactful news you can use.
